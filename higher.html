HIGHER SECONDARY SCHOOLING - Ⅻ

I completed my higher secondary education at Edustar International School, following the CBSE curriculum. I secured a score of 90%, which, to be honest, I find a bit hard to celebrate. Being part of the "Corona batch" meant navigating an unprecedented educational landscape, where traditional learning was disrupted and replaced with online classes and virtual assessments. While I recognize that achieving 90% is commendable, the circumstances surrounding my education during the pandemic have left me feeling that my efforts were somewhat overshadowed by the challenges we faced. Nevertheless, I am determined to use this experience as motivation to excel in my future endeavors and prove that I can achieve even greater things ahead.
